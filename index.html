some of my favorite movies recently

The Grand Budapest Hotel (2014)

comedy/adventure, it makes you think. i love this movie, from the filmmaking to the plot itself. i would recommend this to anyone who loves an interesting storyline and the art noveau era. my top favorite!

93% on rotten tomatoes

Little Miss Sunshine (2006)

comedy/drama, i loved the character dynamics in this film.. overall such a nice watch. sometimes sad, sometimes funny, and leaves you with a comforted feeling. i need to rewatch it soon, and i would recommend this to anyone!

91% on rotten tomatoes

Almost Famous (2000)

such a great movie, i loved the soundtrack, the plot, and the acting was really good. great for those who love music, particularly 70s rock.
